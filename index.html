Pho Plus

Vietnamese

Pho Plus is a Center Street favorite. They serve massive bowls loaded with noodles, all kinds of meat, and even a generous side of optional add ins for no extra charge.

Aloha Plate

Hawaiian

Aloha Plate is located in the Wilkinson Center on BYU campus. They serve the best katsu chicken in the state, with the ability to purchase in a "keiki" size for a screaming deal for students on a budget.

Brigham Young University, Ernest L. Wilkinson Student Center, Cougareat Food Court Suite 2270, Provo, UT 84602
